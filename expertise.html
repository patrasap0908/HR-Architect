Our Expertise

Employee Engagement

According to Gallup's Global workplace report, only 15% of employees worldwide are engaged in their jobs. Majority of employees (67%) are not engaged and still worse, 18% of employees are actively disengaged.

The HR Architect can help you deploy an effective Employee Engagement strategy to realise Employee Productivity to a greater proportion.

High-Performance Work Culture.

A powerful Performance Management System that is highly objective in nature and KPI driven helps in linking it to a strong Reward and Recognition Programme. It facilitates to differentiate and usher a work culture of Pay for Performance.

The HR Architect can strengthen your existing Performance Management System and/or completely design and deploy a new PMS that will promote a High-Performance Work Culture.

Talent Engagement, Development and Management Solutions.

Research on cost of replacement-recruitment is as high as 300% of the annual salary of the departing employee. Hence, the focus of companies should be on how to retain the employees, especially the key talent holding business critical positions to ensure continuity and growth.

The HR Architect offers path-breaking Talent Management and Development Solutions tailored to meet your specific needs.

Learning and Development, Knowledge Management and OD Interventions

L&D initiatives fuelled with inputs received from the PMS help build up skills and competencies those are relevant to the current and future roles of employees. OD and Change Management initiatives deployed time to time to enhance the organisational effectiveness is key to business growth and renewal.

The HR Architect offers customised solutions to enhance the effectiveness at four different levels, i.e., Individual Employees, the dyad (superior - subordinate), the Functional Teams and the Cross-Functional Teams (CFTs), thereby addressing the overall effectiveness of your organisation.
